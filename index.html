Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any software?
No! StockAI-Pro runs entirely in your browser. The mobile app installs directly from the browser — no app store needed.
Is it only for restaurants?
Not at all! StockAI-Pro works for any business that holds stock — restaurants, retail, manufacturers, pharmacies, warehouses, and more.
How does the live food cost work?
Food cost is calculated in real-time: (Opening Stock + Purchases - Closing Stock) / Sales. All values are calculated excluding VAT. You always know exactly where you stand.
Can I control who sees what?
Yes! Create custom roles with specific permissions. Each user only sees the apps and pages you assign to them. You can also control which apps appear in their Hub.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, daily backups, and are fully POPIA compliant. Data is stored securely in Google Firebase.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.
