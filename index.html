Everything you need to know before getting started

How does the 30% launch special work?
The launch special gives the first 20 customers 30% off their monthly subscription. Once 20 customers have signed up, the special automatically falls away. Submit your details now to claim your spot.
How do I get started?
Simply fill in the form above with your details. Our team will review your requirements and send you a personalised quote link within 24 hours. No commitment required — just tell us what you need.
Do I need to install any software?
No! StockAI-Pro runs entirely in your browser. The mobile app installs directly — no app store needed.
How does Nikki work?
Just say "Hey Nikki" on any device. She is powered by OpenAI GPT-4o and has access to your actual live business data — stock levels, sales, food cost, staff clocked in and more. She responds verbally in your language.
Is it only for restaurants?
Not at all! StockAI-Pro works for any business that holds stock — restaurants, retail, manufacturers, pharmacies, warehouses.

POS Builder lets you configure your POS menus and categories. CashUp-Pro handles manager cash-up and reconciliation. Both are included FREE when you purchase any POS system (Restaurant or Retail).
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Bank-level encryption, daily backups, POPIA compliant. Data stored securely in Google Firebase.

Yes — no contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. 30-day money-back guarantee included.
