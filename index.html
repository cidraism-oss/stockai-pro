Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

Do I need to install any software?
No! StockAI-Pro runs entirely in your browser. The mobile app installs directly — no app store needed.
Is it only for restaurants?
Not at all! StockAI-Pro works for any business that holds stock — restaurants, retail, manufacturers, pharmacies, warehouses.
How does Nikki work?
Just say "Hey Nikki" on any device. She is powered by OpenAI GPT-4o and has access to your actual live business data — stock levels, sales, food cost, staff clocked in and more. She responds verbally in your language.
How does the live food cost work?
Food cost is calculated in real-time: (Opening Stock + Purchases - Closing Stock) / Sales. All values excluding VAT.
What is POS Builder and CashUp-Pro?
POS Builder lets you configure your POS menus and categories. CashUp-Pro handles manager cash-up and reconciliation. Both are included FREE when you purchase any POS system (Restaurant or Retail).
Can I manage events and functions?
Yes! FunctionsAI lets you create full event sheets with client details, menu selection, beverage packages, auto-pricing, and professional invoices with signature lines.
What does the HR Module include?
The HR Module includes staff clock in/out, leave management, shift scheduling and payslip generation — all in one place for R349/month.
Can I control who sees what?
Yes! Create custom roles with specific permissions. Each user only sees the apps and pages you assign.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Bank-level encryption, daily backups, POPIA compliant. Data stored securely in Google Firebase.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. 30-day money-back guarantee included.
Can I choose which features I pay for?
Yes! With our "Build Your Base" model, you only pay for the features you select. Web App B.O.H is always included as a flat base fee, and you can add any of the 13 available apps as needed.
