Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any software?
No! StockAI-Pro runs entirely in your web browser. Just sign up, log in, and start managing. The mobile app installs directly from the browser — no app store needed.
Is it only for restaurants?
Not at all! StockAI-Pro works for any business that holds stock — restaurants, retail stores, supermarkets, manufacturers, pharmacies, warehouses, and more. If you track stock, StockAI-Pro saves you money.
How does the 14-day free trial work?
Sign up and get full access to all features for 14 days — no credit card required. At the end of your trial you can choose a plan and continue, or simply cancel with no obligation.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, daily backups, and are fully POPIA compliant. Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase data centres and never shared with third parties.
When will the POS and Procurement App be available?
Both are currently in development and will be released in the coming months. Premium Plus and Premium Exclusive subscribers will get automatic access at no extra cost the moment they launch.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can change plans anytime from your customer portal. A 30-day notice period applies for downgrades. Upgrades take effect immediately.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no penalties. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with 30 days notice. Your data remains accessible until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export all your data at any time in CSV or PDF format. After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days before being permanently deleted.
